# Data Scientist

Company: DataCorp Analytics

Job Title: Data Scientist

We are looking for a Data Scientist to extract insights from complex datasets.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to drive business decisions.
- Build predictive models and machine learning systems.
- Communicate findings to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Required Skills:
- Python
- SQL
- Pandas
- NumPy
- R

Preferred Skills:
- PyTorch
- Tableau
- GCP

Experience:
- Minimum 4 years of experience as a Data Scientist or Data Analyst.

Education:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, or Computer Science.

Certifications:
- Google Cloud Certified Data Engineer is preferred.
